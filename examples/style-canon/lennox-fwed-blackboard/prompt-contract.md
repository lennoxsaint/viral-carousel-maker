# Lennox/Fwed ImageGen Prompt Contract

Use this contract for Lennox Saint's personal Viral Carousel Maker production images.

## Non-negotiables

- Final image path: ImageGen production, not code-rendered final slides.
- Handle: exact text `@lennox_saint` bottom-left on every slide.
- Background: deep true-black chalkboard unless Lennox explicitly asks for the white paper variant.
- Fwed: cute red Threadify bear protagonist, white muzzle, glossy black eyes, rounded plush chalk/storybook body.
- Lennox: tight red t-shirt, thin black soft-octagonal glasses, silver chain, vertical rectangle pendant, dark messy hair, moustache.
- Text: titles are chunky readable chalk caps; supporting notes are raw, imperfect, handwritten, human, uneven, but legible.
- Hook: create curiosity and emotional stakes before teaching.
- CTA/comment engine: end with a self-diagnosis prompt whenever the goal is comments.

## Default slide sequence

1. Nobody saw Fwed's idea.
2. Lennox found the lever.
3. Fwed built a bridge.
4. The idea got a pocket.
5. The idea found doors.
6. The moral.

## Comment-maximizing ending

Use one of these when the post goal is comments:

- "What is your post missing: tension, replies, or save value?"
- "Which part breaks first for you: the hook, the reply surface, or the save value?"
- "Would you rather make people agree, argue, or save?"

## Rejection triggers

Regenerate if any slide has:

- wrong handle
- hoodie or loose top on Lennox
- wrong glasses
- missing necklace pendant
- Fwed rendered as a generic bear or pixel-only sprite
- typed-looking subtext
- unreadable handwriting
- no story continuity
- weak slide-1 curiosity
- generic social media template energy
