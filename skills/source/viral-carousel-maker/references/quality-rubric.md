# Quality rubric

Score before rendering. Revise if the average is below `8.5/10` or any category is below `7/10`.

- Hook strength: does slide 1 stop a fast feed scroll?
- Curiosity gap: does each body slide make the next slide feel worth swiping to?
- Saveability: would the target viewer want to come back to this?
- Specificity: are claims concrete instead of generic?
- Clarity: is every slide understandable on mobile?
- Proof integrity: are stats and strong claims supported?
- Visual fit: does the chosen template match the topic and user taste?
- CTA fit: does the final ask feel earned?

Common revisions:

- Replace broad claims with specific viewer pain.
- Cut body slides to one main idea per slide.
- Turn generic tips into named steps or examples.
- Move proof closer to the claim it supports.
- Make the CTA softer if the carousel has not earned a hard ask.
